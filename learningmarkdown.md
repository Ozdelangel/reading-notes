## What is markdown?

**Markdown** is a way to style text on the web using syntax. You control the display of the documents. 
whether it is bold, italic, and you can insert quotes and even images.
### Examples of Markdown
Syntax | Outcome
------------ | -------------
###|header
*** | **bold**
_ _ | _italics_
